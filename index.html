About riadOS

riadOS is a minimal terminal-based operating system that only runs two apps: *Hello App* and *Calculator*. There are no fancy graphics or designs—just text.

This OS is just for fun, and while it might not do much, it's a start. If you're into minimalism, maybe this is the OS for you!
